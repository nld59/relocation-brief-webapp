# Relocation Brief — Brussels, Belgium

## Client profile
Couple looking to buy an apartment in Brussels with a budget of 490000–895000 EUR. Priorities include cafes, restaurants, family-friendly areas, and quiet residential neighborhoods.

## Must-have
- Apartment with 1+ room
- Quiet residential neighborhood
- Cafes and brunch spots nearby
- Parks and green areas available

## Nice-to-have
- Proximity to restaurants and dining options
- Access to public transit
- Family-friendly activities and facilities
- Good local schools

## Red flags
- High noise levels in busy areas
- Rising property prices impacting budget
- Limited green spaces in some districts
- Long commute times to work

## Trade-offs
- Quiet residential areas might lack dining options
- Family-friendly neighborhoods may have higher prices
- Parks might be farther from cafes
- Desirable districts could stretch budget limits
