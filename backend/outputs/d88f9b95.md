# Relocation Brief — Brussels, Belgium

## Client profile
Couple looking to buy an apartment in Brussels. Budget is 770,000–1,055,000 EUR. Priorities include restaurants, central access, and cafes & brunch.

## Must-have
- 1+ room apartment in Brussels
- Proximity to public transport
- Access to local restaurants
- Availability of cafes and brunch spots
- Family-friendly environment

## Nice-to-have
- Balcony or outdoor space
- Modern kitchen amenities
- Parking availability
- Low maintenance fees
- Close to parks and green spaces

## Red flags
- High noise levels from nearby bars
- Limited public transport options in some districts
- Long commute times during peak hours
- Risk of budget overruns on property taxes
- Potential hidden costs in the buying process

## Trade-offs
- Desire for central access may limit budget
- Preference for modern amenities vs. older buildings
- Restaurants may mean busier neighborhoods
- Cafes might not fit in quieter areas
