# Relocation Brief — Brussels, Belgium

## Client profile
Couple looking to buy an apartment in Brussels. Budget is 490000–895000 EUR. Priorities include cafes, family-friendliness, and expat community.

## Must-have
- 1+ room apartment type
- Access to family-friendly parks
- Proximity to cafes and brunch spots
- Community services for expats
- Public transport access

## Nice-to-have
- Modern apartment amenities
- Outdoor space or balcony
- Low-rise buildings preferred
- Secure building access
- Walking distance to schools

## Red flags
- High maintenance fees for older buildings
- Limited parking space availability
- Potential noise from busy streets
- Overpriced listings in popular areas
- Prolonged closing or negotiation processes

## Trade-offs
- Expansive living space vs. budget constraints
- Quiet neighborhood vs. proximity to cafes
- New developments vs. older, character-filled apartments
- Family-friendly areas vs. vibrant nightlife

## Top-3 areas (shortlist)
### 1) Ixelles
**Scorecard (1–5):** Safety:4 | Family:5 | Commute:4 | Lifestyle:3 | BudgetFit:3 | Overall:4

**Why:**
- Home to lively cafes like Place Flagey and vibrant markets.
- Strong expat community with easy access to public transport options.

**Watch-out:**
- Higher property prices may stretch the budget significantly.

### 2) Saint-Gilles
**Scorecard (1–5):** Safety:3 | Family:4 | Commute:3 | Lifestyle:3 | BudgetFit:3 | Overall:3

**Why:**
- Features beautiful parks like Parc du Duden, ideal for families.
- A mix of international eateries and lively brunch spots enhances lifestyle.

**Watch-out:**
- Traffic congestion during peak hours can prolong commute times.

### 3) Uccle
**Scorecard (1–5):** Safety:5 | Family:5 | Commute:3 | Lifestyle:3 | BudgetFit:3 | Overall:4

**Why:**
- Offers spacious family homes near green spaces and playgrounds.
- Cafes and markets provide a robust expat and local experience.

**Watch-out:**
- Further from the city center can impact daily commute choices.

## Essentials to ask your Real Estate agent
- What is the total cost including fees and taxes?
- What are the common building charges and works planned?
- Can you provide recent inspection reports or evaluations?
- What is the process for contract negotiation?
- How long is the expected timeline for closing?
