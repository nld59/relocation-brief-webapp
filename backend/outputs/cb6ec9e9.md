# Relocation Brief — Brussels, Belgium

## Client profile
Couple looking to rent an apartment in Brussels. Budget is 2050–3600 EUR/month. Priorities are cafes & brunch, restaurants, and local market feel.

## Executive summary (quick scan)
| Commune | Best for | Watch-outs | Top microhoods |
|---|---|---|---|
| Etterbeek | Strong international / expat ecosystem and services. | Prime pockets can be pricey; validate the full monthly cost. | Jourdan · Saint - Pierre |
| Schaerbeek | Balanced option around Schaerbeek with a clear trade-off between space, commute and amenities. | Busier evenings in hotspots; confirm noise levels during a late walk-through. | Collignon · Dailly |
| City of Brussels | Stronger green pockets around Grand Place. | Traffic/noise can be noticeable on main arteries; shortlist street-by-street. | Grand Place · Notre - Dame aux Neiges |

## Must-have
- Minimum 2 rooms in the apartment.
- Proximity to cafes and restaurants.
- Access to local markets.

## Nice-to-have
- Nearby parks or green spaces.
- Art and cultural venues in the area.
- Good public transport links.

## Red flags
- Higher rental prices near popular cafe areas.
- Loud traffic from main streets.
- Limited parking availability in urban zones.

## Trade-offs
- High demand in central areas plus limited parking.
- Expensive rentals with local market vibe.
- Quiet neighborhoods vs. vibrant cafes.

## Next steps
- Shortlist 8–12 listings across the 3 communes and set up viewings.
- Confirm total monthly cost: rent + charges + utilities (and what's included).
- Validate commute: run one test route at peak hours (public transport and by car if relevant).
- Ask about parking rules/permits and bike/storage options.
- Prepare a rental document pack: ID, proof of income, employer letter, bank statements.
- For the top 2 options, do an evening walk-through to assess noise and safety.
- Clarify contract length, notice period, indexation, and deposit rules.
- Book a second visit with measurements and photos to compare objectively.
- Pre-validate a rental guarantee to move fast on good listings.
- Confirm handover checklist and inventory (appliances/fixtures) in writing.
