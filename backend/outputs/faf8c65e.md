# Relocation Brief — Brussels, Belgium

## Client profile
Family seeking a premium home in Brussels, budget 760,000-1,175,000 EUR. Priorities include family-friendly environment and proximity to cafes.

## Must-have
- Minimum of 3 rooms
- Family-friendly areas
- Quiet residential neighborhoods
- Access to schools within 20 min walk
- Proximity to cafes and brunch spots

## Nice-to-have
- Garden or outdoor space
- Modern amenities and finishes
- Good public transport access
- Secure building or area
- Community activities for families

## Red flags
- High noise levels near busy areas
- Limited school options within walking distance
- Exceeding budget for preferred districts
- Long commutes affecting family time
- Poor conditions in older buildings

## Trade-offs
- Desire for quiet but also close to cafes
- Premium feel might stretch budget limits
- Family-friendly areas may be busier
- Proximity to schools but needing quiet zones

## Top-3 areas (shortlist)
### 1) Ixelles
**Scorecard (1–5):** Safety:4 | Family:5 | Commute:3 | Lifestyle:3 | BudgetFit:3 | Overall:4

**Why:**
- Home to Place Flagey, a lively square with cafes and parks.
- Parc du Chatelain offers green space, ideal for families.

**Watch-out:**
- Traffic congestion can affect commute times during peak hours.

### 2) Uccle
**Scorecard (1–5):** Safety:5 | Family:5 | Commute:4 | Lifestyle:3 | BudgetFit:3 | Overall:4

**Why:**
- Parc de Woluwé provides ample recreational options for children.
- Quieter streets ensure a peaceful living environment.

**Watch-out:**
- Higher property prices may limit choices within the budget.

### 3) Etterbeek
**Scorecard (1–5):** Safety:4 | Family:5 | Commute:4 | Lifestyle:3 | BudgetFit:3 | Overall:4

**Why:**
- Proximity to Parc du Cinquantenaire, ideal for family outings.
- Square de la Chasse offers a tranquil neighborhood feel.

**Watch-out:**
- Rapid development could impact local housing prices.

## Essentials to ask your Real Estate agent
- What is the total deposit required and are there additional fees?
- What utilities are included and which are separate?
- What is the length of the lease and termination terms?
- Are there any rules regarding noise or pets?
- How often does the rent increase and how is it indexed?

## Clarifying questions (if needed)
- Are there specific schools preferred?
- What is the desired timeline for moving?
- Is there a preference for new or older properties?
