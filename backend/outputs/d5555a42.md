# Relocation Brief — Brussels, Belgium

## Client profile
A couple looking to buy an apartment in Brussels. Budget is EUR 490,000 to 895,000. Priorities include cafes, parks, and EU quarter proximity.

## Must-have
- 1+ room apartment
- Access to parks and green areas
- Proximity to cafes and brunch spots
- Easy commute to the EU quarter
- Safe neighborhood

## Nice-to-have
- Modern amenities
- Balcony or outdoor space
- Public transport access
- Low maintenance fees
- Pet-friendly policies

## Red flags
- High noise levels in certain districts
- Potentially expensive renovation costs
- Limited parking availability
- Long commutes in some areas
- Excessive property taxes

## Trade-offs
- Budget constraints versus desired amenities
- Proximity to lively cafes versus tranquility
- Modern space requirements versus historic charm
- Lifestyle preferences versus commuting times

**Watch-out:**
- High property prices may stretch your budget significantly.
