# Relocation Brief — Brussels, Belgium

## Client profile
A couple looking to buy an apartment in Brussels with a budget of 490,000–895,000 EUR. Priorities include cafes, expat community, value for money, and a residential, quiet environment.

## Must-have
- 2+ rooms in an apartment
- Proximity to cafes and brunch spots
- Access to expat community resources
- Quiet residential area
- Value for money in housing

## Nice-to-have
- Near parks or green spaces
- Public transport accessibility
- Easy access to supermarkets
- Local restaurants and shops
- Community events or gatherings

## Red flags
- High noise levels in certain areas
- Potential for budget limits to stretch
- Long commutes due to location
- Limited parking options
- Extreme property price fluctuations

## Trade-offs
- Quiet living may limit cafe options
- Desirable expat areas can be expensive
- Residential zones might have fewer brunch spots
- Value for money may compromise space
