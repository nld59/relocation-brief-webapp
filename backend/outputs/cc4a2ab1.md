# Relocation Brief — Brussels, Belgium

## Client profile
Couple looking to buy an apartment in Brussels. Budget is 490000–895000 EUR. Priorities include cafes, family-friendly amenities, and proximity to the EU quarter.

## Must-have
- 1+ room apartment
- Access to cafes and brunch spots
- Family-friendly environment
- Proximity to international community
- Good public transport access to EU quarter

## Nice-to-have
- Green spaces nearby
- Modern amenities in the apartment
- Active expat community
- Low maintenance fees
- Grocery stores within walking distance

## Red flags
- High property taxes that could impact budget
- Limited parking availability in popular districts
- Potential noise from busy areas or nightlife
- Buildings requiring major repairs or upgrades
- Rising property values affecting long-term affordability

## Trade-offs
- Desire for family-friendly yet vibrant districts
- Looking for cafes but want quiet living space
- Interest in expat areas but concerns about costs
- Preference for modern amenities versus older charm

## Top-3 areas (shortlist)
### 1) Ixelles
**Scorecard (1–5):** Safety:4 | Family:5 | Commute:4 | Lifestyle:3 | BudgetFit:3 | Overall:4

**Why:**
- Rich in cafes and cultural spots like Flagey Square, perfect for brunch lovers.
- Family-friendly parks such as Parc Tenbosch offer space for outdoor activities.

**Watch-out:**
- Noise levels may rise during weekends due to nightlife in the area.

### 2) European Quarter
**Scorecard (1–5):** Safety:5 | Family:4 | Commute:5 | Lifestyle:3 | BudgetFit:3 | Overall:4

**Why:**
- Proximity to the EU institutions ensures easy access for professionals.
- Pleasant cafes along Rue de la Loi provide a vibrant atmosphere for expats.

**Watch-out:**
- Higher property prices could exceed initial budget expectations.

### 3) Saint-Gilles
**Scorecard (1–5):** Safety:3 | Family:4 | Commute:3 | Lifestyle:3 | BudgetFit:3 | Overall:3

**Why:**
- Diverse community with cafes near Parvis de Saint-Gilles, appealing to expats.
- Eco-friendly initiatives and parks promote a family-oriented lifestyle.

**Watch-out:**
- Busy roads can cause challenges with noise and parking logistics.
