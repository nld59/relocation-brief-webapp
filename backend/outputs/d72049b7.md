# Relocation Brief — Brussels

## Client profile
Couple seeking to buy an apartment in Brussels. Budget is 790,000-1,260,000 EUR. Priorities include restaurants, cafes, and residential quiet.

## Must-have
- 2+ rooms in an apartment
- Safe and calm neighborhood
- Access to restaurants and cafes

## Nice-to-have
- Proximity to public transport
- Green spaces nearby
- Value-for-money options available

## Red flags
- High noise from busy streets
- Limited parking availability
- Potential budget stretch in prime areas

## Trade-offs
- Desire for quiet versus proximity to lively cafes
- Focus on value versus high-end properties
- Preference for central access versus quieter outskirts

## Top-3 areas (shortlist)
### 1) Ixelles
**Scorecard (1–5):** Safety:3 | Family:2 | Commute:4 | Lifestyle:3 | BudgetFit:3 | Overall:3

**Why:**
- Châtelain offers vibrant cafes and brunch spots, attracting expats and young professionals.
- Flagey features shops and a lively public square, enhancing community interaction.

**Watch-out:**
- Popular streets experience noise on weekends, and parking is often tight.

### 2) Etterbeek
**Scorecard (1–5):** Safety:4 | Family:4 | Commute:4 | Lifestyle:3 | BudgetFit:3 | Overall:4

**Why:**
- Jourdan district has diverse restaurants and cafes, popular with young professionals.
- Cinquantenaire park provides green space and family-friendly activities within reach.

**Watch-out:**
- Rents near the EU quarter can be higher, potentially stretching the budget.

### 3) Saint-Gilles
**Scorecard (1–5):** Safety:3 | Family:2 | Commute:4 | Lifestyle:3 | BudgetFit:3 | Overall:3

**Why:**
- Parvis de Saint-Gilles offers trendy brunch spots and a vibrant art scene.
- Barriere features eclectic shops integrated into a lively neighborhood atmosphere.

**Watch-out:**
- Some streets can be noisy late; choose quieter side streets to relax.

## Essentials to ask your Real Estate agent
- What is the total cost including taxes and fees?
- Are there any building charges or planned works?
- What is the condition of the property assessed?
- Can you provide a timeline for the purchase process?
- Are there any legal compliance issues I should be aware of?

## Clarifying questions (if needed)
- What is the preferred move-in date?
- Are there specific amenities or features required?
- Is there flexibility on the budget range?
