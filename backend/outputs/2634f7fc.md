# Relocation Brief — Brussels, Belgium

## Client profile
A couple seeking to buy an apartment in Brussels, with a budget of 490000–895000 EUR. Priorities include cafes, restaurants, and expat-friendly areas.

## Must-have
- 1+ room apartment
- Access to public transportation
- Proximity to cafes and restaurants
- Modern amenities
- Safe neighborhood

## Nice-to-have
- Balcony or outdoor space
- Near parks or green areas
- International schools nearby
- Parking availability
- Access to cultural attractions

## Red flags
- High competition in popular neighborhoods
- Potential for budget overruns
- Noise levels in busy areas
- Long commute times from outskirts
- Limited availability of spacious apartments

## Trade-offs
- Family-friendly areas may stretch the budget
- Busy urban districts might lack tranquility
- Expats may prefer areas with higher costs
- Desired amenities may limit property options

## Top-3 areas (shortlist)
### 1) Ixelles
**Scorecard (1–5):** Safety:4 | Family:3 | Commute:4 | Lifestyle:3 | BudgetFit:3 | Overall:3

**Why:**
- Ixelles offers vibrant cafes and restaurants, especially on Parvis de Flagey and Rue de Flore.
- The area has a strong expat community supported by cultural events and language schools.

**Watch-out:**
- Noise from nightlife can impact peaceful living, particularly on weekends.

### 2) Saint-Gilles
**Scorecard (1–5):** Safety:4 | Family:3 | Commute:4 | Lifestyle:3 | BudgetFit:3 | Overall:3

**Why:**
- Saint-Gilles boasts trendy brunch spots, including those near Avenue Louise and Place Van Meenen.
- The district is home to many multinational residents, enhancing its international vibe.

**Watch-out:**
- Property prices are rising quickly, making budgeting crucial for potential buyers.

### 3) Schaerbeek
**Scorecard (1–5):** Safety:4 | Family:5 | Commute:3 | Lifestyle:3 | BudgetFit:3 | Overall:4

**Why:**
- Schaerbeek has lovely parks, such as Josaphat Park, ideal for family outings and leisure.
- The district features a diverse array of eateries and is known for its vibrant local culture.

**Watch-out:**
- Commute times can be longer if living far from transit options.
