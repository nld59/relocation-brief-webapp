# Relocation Brief — Brussels, Belgium

## Client profile
Family with 1 child in Brussels looking to buy a home. Budget is 760,000 to 1,175,000 EUR. Priorities include premium feel, residential atmosphere, and family-friendly amenities.

## Must-have
- Minimum 3 bedrooms
- Quiet residential area
- Access to schools within 20 min walk
- Proximity to cafes and social spots
- Family-friendly parks and playgrounds

## Nice-to-have
- Modern amenities and finishes
- Access to public transport
- Green spaces nearby
- Shopping areas within 15 min
- Community events and activities

## Red flags
- High property taxes could exceed budget
- Limited school options may affect commute
- Home repairs might add unexpected costs
- High competition for family-friendly homes
- Noise from nearby main roads or constructions

## Trade-offs
- Family-friendly areas may stretch budget
- Premium properties could compromise space
- Quiet neighborhoods might limit amenities
- Close to schools could mean noisy environment

## Top-3 areas (shortlist)
### 1) Ixelles
**Scorecard (1–5):** Safety:4 | Family:5 | Commute:4 | Lifestyle:3 | BudgetFit:3 | Overall:4

**Why:**
- Ixelles offers vibrant cafes around Flagey Square and peaceful parks like Parc Tenbosch.
- Excellent schools nearby make it appealing for families looking for a community feel.

**Watch-out:**
- Busy streets can lead to noise disturbances in certain areas.

### 2) Uccle
**Scorecard (1–5):** Safety:5 | Family:5 | Commute:3 | Lifestyle:3 | BudgetFit:3 | Overall:4

**Why:**
- Uccle features spacious homes near Parc Brugmann, ideal for families seeking outdoor space.
- Quiet streets and good schools make it attractive for family living.

**Watch-out:**
- Home prices can be higher than in nearby areas, affecting budget.

### 3) Woluwe-Saint-Pierre
**Scorecard (1–5):** Safety:5 | Family:5 | Commute:3 | Lifestyle:3 | BudgetFit:3 | Overall:4

**Why:**
- This district provides ample green spaces like Parc de Woluwe, perfect for children.
- Family-friendly facilities and schools make it a convenient choice for parents.

**Watch-out:**
- Limited shopping options may require longer commutes for essentials.


## Next steps
- Schedule viewings in preferred districts.
- Research schools based on priority needs.
- Obtain financing pre-approval for budget range.
- Explore local amenities for family activities.

## Essentials to ask your Real Estate agent
- What is the total purchase price including fees?
- Are there any pending building works on the property?
- What is the property tax situation in this area?
- How is the neighborhood during weekends or evenings?
- Are there schools within walking distance?
