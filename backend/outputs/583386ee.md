# Relocation Brief — Brussels, Belgium

## Client profile
Couple looking to buy an apartment in Brussels with a budget of 490000–895000 EUR. Priorities include cafes, expat community, and value for money.

## Must-have
- 1+ room apartment
- Good access to public transport
- Proximity to cafes and brunch spots
- Safe neighborhood
- Pet-friendly options

## Nice-to-have
- Access to parks and green spaces
- Modern amenities
- Balcony or terrace
- Walkable neighborhood
- International schools nearby

## Red flags
- High property taxes in certain areas
- Noise from nearby bars or restaurants
- Local crime rates fluctuating
- Potential renovation costs
- Limited parking availability

## Trade-offs
- Value for money vs. premium locations
- Access to cafes vs. quiet neighborhoods
- Size vs. location considerations
- Modern amenities vs. historic buildings

## Top-3 areas (shortlist)
### 1) Ixelles
**Scorecard (1–5):** Safety:4 | Family:4 | Commute:5 | Lifestyle:3 | BudgetFit:3 | Overall:4

**Why:**
- Home to lively neighborhoods and abundant cafes, ideal for brunch and socializing.
- Strong expat community with English-speaking services along Avenue Louise.

**Watch-out:**
- Property prices can exceed budget, especially near main attractions.

### 2) Saint-Gilles
**Scorecard (1–5):** Safety:3 | Family:3 | Commute:5 | Lifestyle:3 | BudgetFit:3 | Overall:3

**Why:**
- Offers a multicultural vibe with many brunch options near Parvis de Saint-Gilles.
- Walking distance to key transport links, facilitating easy commuting.

**Watch-out:**
- Renovation projects can affect property prices and timelines.

### 3) Etterbeek
**Scorecard (1–5):** Safety:4 | Family:5 | Commute:4 | Lifestyle:3 | BudgetFit:3 | Overall:4

**Why:**
- Features numerous parks, great for outdoor cafes and leisure activities.
- Close proximity to European institutions, attracting international residents.

**Watch-out:**
- Commute time can increase during rush hours due to traffic.

## Essentials to ask your Real Estate agent
- What is the total deposit required and any additional fees?
- Are utilities included in the monthly rent or paid separately?
- What are the terms for lease termination or renewal?
- Are there any rules regarding noise or shared spaces?
- How is rent indexed or registered?

## Clarifying questions (if needed)
- What is your preferred living style?
- Are you open to fixer-upper apartments?
- How soon do you want to move?
