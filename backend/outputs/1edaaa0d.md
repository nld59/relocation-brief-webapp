# Relocation Brief — Brussels, Belgium

## Client profile
Family of three looking to buy a house in Brussels. Budget is EUR 770,000–1,080,000. Priorities include cafes, parks, and quiet residential areas.

## Must-have
- 2+ bedrooms for family comfort.
- Access to parks and green spaces.
- Proximity to international cafes.
- Quiet residential environment.
- School accessibility within 20 min walk.

## Nice-to-have
- Easy commute to the EU quarter.
- Close to public transportation.
- Community of expats nearby.
- Child-friendly amenities and facilities.
- Access to local markets and shops.

## Red flags
- High property taxes may affect budget.
- Limited availability of desired properties.
- Potential noise in central locations.
- Long commute times from outer areas.
- Maintenance costs could be high.

## Trade-offs
- Quiet zones may lack cafe options.
- Parks may increase distance to EU access.
- Top schools might be in busier areas.
- Expats areas can be pricier.

## Top-3 areas (shortlist)
### 1) Ixelles
**Scorecard (1–5):** Safety:4 | Family:5 | Commute:4 | Lifestyle:3 | BudgetFit:3 | Overall:4

**Why:**
- Features numerous parks like Flagey Square, ideal for family outings and activities.
- Diverse cafes create a vibrant brunch scene, attracting many international residents.

**Watch-out:**
- Parking can be challenging due to local events and high traffic.

### 2) Etterbeek
**Scorecard (1–5):** Safety:5 | Family:5 | Commute:5 | Lifestyle:3 | BudgetFit:3 | Overall:4

**Why:**
- Close to Parc du Cinquantenaire offering greenery and picnic areas for families.
- Attracts expats due to proximity to EU institutions and international schools.

**Watch-out:**
- Prices can stretch the upper limit of your budget in desirable areas.

### 3) Saint-Gilles
**Scorecard (1–5):** Safety:3 | Family:4 | Commute:4 | Lifestyle:3 | BudgetFit:3 | Overall:3

**Why:**
- Known for its local cafes, providing a vibrant culture right in the neighborhood.
- Access to public transport allows for easy commutes throughout Brussels.

**Watch-out:**
- Higher noise levels may occur during weekends due to local nightlife.
