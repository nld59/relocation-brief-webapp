# Relocation Brief — Brussels, Belgium

## Client profile
Couple looking to buy an apartment in Brussels. Budget is 490,000–895,000 EUR. Priorities include cafes, restaurants, and family-friendly green areas.

## Must-have
- 1+ room apartment
- Quiet residential area
- Proximity to parks
- Access to public transport

## Nice-to-have
- Cafes and brunch spots nearby
- Restaurants within walking distance
- Family-friendly schools close by

## Red flags
- High property taxes
- Areas with noise issues
- Limited green space availability

## Trade-offs
- Prefer quiet but want near lively cafes
- Family-friendly yet looking for vibrant lifestyle areas
