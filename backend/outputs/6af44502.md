# Relocation Brief — Brussels, Belgium

## Client profile
A couple looking to buy an apartment in Brussels. Budget ranges from 490,000 to 895,000 EUR. Priorities include cafes, family-friendliness, and access to the EU quarter.

## Must-have
- 1+ room apartments.
- Proximity to brunch spots and cafes.
- Family-friendly neighborhood features.
- Access to public transport.
- Expats-friendly community.

## Nice-to-have
- Green spaces or parks nearby.
- Local markets for fresh produce.
- Community events for expats.
- Cultural venues and museums.
- Good schools within walking distance.

## Red flags
- Limited parking availability.
- High noise levels in busy areas.
- Budget constraints from rising costs.
- Difficulties in finding suitable listings.
- Potential long commute to the EU quarter.

## Trade-offs
- Family-friendly areas may lack nightlife.
- Expats neighborhoods can be higher in price.
- Some cafes may not cater to families.
- Central locations may result in more noise.

## Top-3 areas (shortlist)
### 1) Ixelles
**Scorecard (1–5):** Safety:4 | Family:5 | Commute:4 | Lifestyle:3 | BudgetFit:3 | Overall:4

**Why:**
- Features vibrant cafes along Flagey Square, perfect for brunch outings.
- Family-friendly parks such as Parc Tenbosch enhance community feel.

**Watch-out:**
- Can experience high noise levels during weekends and events.

### 2) Saint-Gilles
**Scorecard (1–5):** Safety:3 | Family:5 | Commute:4 | Lifestyle:3 | BudgetFit:3 | Overall:4

**Why:**
- Boasts many expat-friendly cafes near Parvis de Saint-Gilles.
- Offers great family-friendly amenities like playgrounds and schools.

**Watch-out:**
- Budget can be stretched due to rising property prices.

### 3) European Quarter
**Scorecard (1–5):** Safety:5 | Family:4 | Commute:5 | Lifestyle:3 | BudgetFit:3 | Overall:4

**Why:**
- Easy access to EU institutions enhances the area's appeal and convenience.
- Nearby restaurants offer diverse brunch options for families and expats.

**Watch-out:**
- Longer commutes during peak office hours might be challenging.
