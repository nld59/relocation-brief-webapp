# Relocation Brief — Brussels, Belgium

## Client profile
Family with 1 child relocating to Brussels, buying an apartment. Budget is 785,000–1,120,000 EUR. Priorities include residential areas, cafes, and parks.

## Must-have
- Minimum of 3 rooms in the apartment
- Proximity to parks and green spaces
- Quiet residential areas
- Walkable distance to schools
- Good cafes and eateries nearby

## Nice-to-have
- Access to public transport
- Parking space included
- Family-friendly community
- Outdoor play areas
- Nearby grocery stores

## Red flags
- High costs in popular districts
- Noise from busy streets
- Limited availability of spacious apartments
- Inadequate school options within a short commute
- Potential property value fluctuations

## Trade-offs
- Quiet living vs. proximity to cafes
- Desire for parks vs. budget constraints
- Need for walkable commute vs. property size
- Family needs vs. luxury housing

## Top-3 areas (shortlist)
### 1) Ixelles
**Scorecard (1–5):** Safety:4 | Family:5 | Commute:4 | Lifestyle:3 | BudgetFit:3 | Overall:4

**Why:**
- Ixelles boasts trendy cafes around Flagey and a vibrant community atmosphere.
- Enjoy parks like Pond de la Cambre for children's play and family activities.

**Watch-out:**
- Consider traffic congestion during rush hours that could affect school commutes.

### 2) Uccle
**Scorecard (1–5):** Safety:5 | Family:5 | Commute:3 | Lifestyle:3 | BudgetFit:3 | Overall:4

**Why:**
- Uccle features excellent parks like Parc de Woluwe, ideal for family outings.
- The district offers a range of premium cafes and a calm environment.

**Watch-out:**
- High property prices in desirable areas might exceed budget limits.

### 3) Etterbeek
**Scorecard (1–5):** Safety:4 | Family:4 | Commute:4 | Lifestyle:3 | BudgetFit:3 | Overall:4

**Why:**
- Etterbeek has family-friendly parks like Parc du Cinquantenaire for weekend activities.
- It's well-connected with cafes and local shops within walking distance.

**Watch-out:**
- Ensure the apartment's noise insulation since some parts can be busier.
