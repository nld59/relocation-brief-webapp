# Relocation Brief — Brussels, Belgium

## Client profile
A couple looking to buy an apartment in Brussels, with a budget of 770,000–1,055,000 EUR. Priorities include restaurants, central access, and cafes & brunch.

## Must-have
- 1+ bedrooms in the apartment.
- Access to public transportation.
- Proximity to restaurants and cafes.
- Safe neighborhood for walking.
- Modern amenities in the apartment.

## Nice-to-have
- Balcony or outdoor space.
- Nearby parks for relaxation.
- Parking options available.
- Proximity to cultural venues.
- Community activities and events.

## Red flags
- High property taxes in certain districts.
- Expensive maintenance fees in older buildings.
- Limited parking spaces in popular areas.
- Noise from nightlife in busy districts.
- Long commute times without good transit.

## Trade-offs
- Desire for central access vs. housing budget.
- Preference for modern apartments vs. older character buildings.
- Focus on cafes vs. need for quiet spaces.
- Interest in nightlife vs. need for family-friendly areas.
