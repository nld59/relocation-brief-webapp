# Relocation Brief — Brussels, Belgium

## Client profile
A couple looking to buy a studio apartment in Brussels, budget 490,000–895,000 EUR. Priorities include cafes & brunch, a residential and quiet environment, and central access.

## Must-have
- Apartment size suitable for a couple
- Access to public transport within 15 min
- Quiet residential area
- Proximity to cafes and brunch spots
- Within budget range of 490,000–895,000 EUR

## Nice-to-have
- Balcony or outdoor space
- Modern appliances installed
- Proximity to parks or green spaces
- High-speed internet access
- Parking space availability

## Red flags
- High property taxes in certain areas
- Lack of parking options
- Limited neighborhood amenities
- High maintenance fees in some buildings
- Potential noise from nearby bars and cafes

## Trade-offs
- Quiet living yet central access can be challenging
- Desire for modern amenities may increase budget
- Residential preference may limit cafe proximity
- Balcony space may be scarce in apartments

## Top-3 areas (shortlist)
### 1) Ixelles
**Scorecard (1–5):** Safety:4 | Family:3 | Commute:5 | Lifestyle:3 | BudgetFit:3 | Overall:4

**Why:**
- Features popular cafes like La Buvette and parks such as Flagey Square for relaxation.
- Excellent public transport connections make commuting easy to central Brussels.

**Watch-out:**
- Property prices can fluctuate based on market trends, impacting your budget.

### 2) Saint-Gilles
**Scorecard (1–5):** Safety:3 | Family:4 | Commute:4 | Lifestyle:3 | BudgetFit:3 | Overall:3

**Why:**
- Charming neighborhood known for its vibrant arts scene and numerous brunch spots.
- Close to public transport providing quick access to the city center.

**Watch-out:**
- Certain areas might become noisy during weekends due to nightlife activities.

### 3) Etterbeek
**Scorecard (1–5):** Safety:4 | Family:5 | Commute:4 | Lifestyle:3 | BudgetFit:3 | Overall:4

**Why:**
- Family-friendly district with parks like Parc du Cinquantenaire for outdoor activities.
- Convenient access to metro stations streamlining your daily commute.

**Watch-out:**
- Potential competition for housing may drive up prices in sought-after spots.
