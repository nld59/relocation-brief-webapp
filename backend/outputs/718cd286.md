# Relocation Brief — Brussels, Belgium

## Client profile
A couple seeking to buy an apartment in Brussels with a budget of 490,000–895,000 EUR. Priorities include cafes & brunch, restaurants, and an international expat community.

## Must-have
- 1+ bedroom apartment
- Proximity to cafes and restaurants
- Access to public transportation
- Safe neighborhood
- Modern amenities

## Nice-to-have
- Balcony or terrace
- Dedicated workspace
- Close to parks
- Low maintenance fees
- Community events

## Red flags
- High property taxes
- Potential for noisy neighbors
- Limited parking spaces
- Long commute times
- Unclear ownership history

## Trade-offs
- High budget may limit options in preferred neighborhoods
- Desire for modern amenities vs. older buildings
- Quiet areas may reduce access to cafes
- Expats-focused areas may have higher prices

## Top-3 areas (shortlist)
### 1) Ixelles
**Scorecard (1–5):** Safety:4 | Family:5 | Commute:4 | Lifestyle:3 | BudgetFit:3 | Overall:4

**Why:**
- Features lively squares like Place Flagey, full of cafes and restaurants.
- Known for its diverse expat community and vibrant cultural events.

**Watch-out:**
- Watch out for high property prices in sought-after streets.

### 2) Saint-Gilles
**Scorecard (1–5):** Safety:3 | Family:4 | Commute:3 | Lifestyle:3 | BudgetFit:3 | Overall:3

**Why:**
- Close to the dynamic Parvis de Saint-Gilles, filled with dining options.
- Home to a growing number of cafes and multicultural activities.

**Watch-out:**
- Parking can be challenging due to high demand in the area.

### 3) Schaerbeek
**Scorecard (1–5):** Safety:4 | Family:5 | Commute:3 | Lifestyle:3 | BudgetFit:3 | Overall:4

**Why:**
- Offers green spaces like Josaphat Park, ideal for relaxation.
- Hosts various expat-friendly events throughout the year.

**Watch-out:**
- Commute times may be longer compared to more central districts.


## Next steps
- Schedule property viewings in top districts.
- Research detailed area statistics and amenities.
- Consult with a local real estate agent.
- Prepare financing options for the purchase.

## Essentials to ask your Real Estate agent
- What are the total costs associated with the purchase?
- Are there any pending legal issues with the property?
- What are the building maintenance fees and known upcoming works?
- Can you provide a recent inspection report for the property?
- What is the typical negotiation timeline for offers?
