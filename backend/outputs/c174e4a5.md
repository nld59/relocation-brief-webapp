# Relocation Brief — Brussels, Belgium

## Client profile
A couple looking to buy a 1+ room apartment in Brussels, budget 490000–895000 EUR. Priorities include cafes, restaurants, family-friendly areas, and a residential, quiet environment.

## Must-have
- 1+ room apartment
- Quiet residential area
- Access to cafes and brunch spots
- Proximity to family-friendly amenities
- Restaurants nearby

## Nice-to-have
- Balcony or terrace
- Parking space
- Good public transport access
- Green spaces nearby
- Modern kitchen amenities

## Red flags
- High noise levels in some areas
- Potential budget stretch in premium districts
- Limited availability of larger apartments
- Property condition can vary significantly
- Long commuting times in outer districts

## Trade-offs
- Desire for quiet but also needs locations with vibrant cafes
- Family-friendly needs may limit proximity to lively restaurants
- Budget constraints might limit desirable residential areas
- Urban lifestyle may collide with preference for spaciousness
