# Relocation Brief — Brussels, Belgium

## Client profile
A couple looking to buy an apartment in Brussels. Budget ranges from 490,000 to 895,000 EUR. Priorities include cafes, restaurants, and parks in a quiet residential area.

## Must-have
- 1+ bedroom apartment
- Proximity to cafes and restaurants
- Access to parks and green areas
- Quiet residential environment
- Public transport accessibility

## Nice-to-have
- Balcony or outdoor space
- Modern kitchen appliances
- Pet-friendly policies
- Proximity to grocery stores
- Community amenities

## Red flags
- High noise levels in some districts
- Limited parking options may exist
- Potential budget overruns during purchase
- Long commutes in certain areas
- Insufficient green spaces nearby

## Trade-offs
- Desire for quiet areas may limit cafe options
- Budget constraints may restrict location choice
- Preference for modern amenities versus older buildings
- Outdoor space versus urban density
