# Relocation Brief — Brussels, Belgium

## Client profile
A couple looking to buy an apartment in Brussels with a budget of 490000–895000 EUR. Priorities include cafes, restaurants, and green areas while seeking a quiet residential lifestyle.

## Must-have
- 1+ room apartment
- Proximity to cafes and brunch spots
- Access to parks and green areas
- Residential and quiet environment
- Public transportation availability

## Nice-to-have
- Balcony or outdoor space
- Modern kitchen and amenities
- Low-maintenance property
- Close to cultural attractions
- Good schools in the area

## Red flags
- High noise levels from busy streets
- Inadequate parking facilities
- Poor public transport connections
- High property taxes in certain areas
- Historical building maintenance issues

## Trade-offs
- Quiet may limit proximity to vibrant districts
- Residential areas may be further from restaurants
- Budget constraints against desired amenities
- Desire for parks could reduce urban choices
